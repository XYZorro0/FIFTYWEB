With over half a century of combined experience, we build Houston's most iconic and elite homes in prestigious neighborhoods including River Oaks and Memorial.

Our Namesake

Inspired by Carnegie Hall

Inspired by the iconic intersection of 57th Street and 7th Avenue in New York City — the home of Carnegie Hall — our name reflects the timeless elegance, grandeur, and cultural significance that Andrew Carnegie's legacy represents.

Just as Carnegie Hall stands as a symbol of artistry and excellence, Fifty Seventh & 7th Luxury Homes embodies those same values in every residence we create.

From Vision to Reality

Arpan Gupta

CEO, Fifty Seventh & 7th Luxury Homes

Arpan Gupta's journey has been anything but conventional. A high school valedictorian, Eagle Scout, and one of just ten students selected for the Rice-Baylor Medical Scholars program, Arpan Gupta was guided early on by a deep respect for learning, service, and excellence.

After earning his doctorate in medicine and joining the Residency program at Baylor College of Medicine, Arpan Gupta made a pivotal choice in 2009 — to step away from medicine and pursue his true passion: building extraordinary homes. A Graduate Master Builder (GMB), he brings the same rigor and dedication to construction that once defined his medical training.

What began with Arpan acting as his own sales agent, superintendent, and project manager has grown into Fifty Seventh & 7th Luxury Homes, a nationally recognized builder known for award-winning design and craftsmanship. His medical training instilled a methodical, detail-driven mindset that now defines his approach to construction.

"My background, where striving for precision is critical, allows us to bring a different perspective to building. Every decision is guided by a commitment to excellence and to creating flawless, personalized designs."

Under Arpan Gupta's leadership, Fifty Seventh & 7th has become a benchmark for design innovation, seamlessly combining clean lines, luxurious materials, and timeless sophistication. He and his team collaborate closely with top architects, designers, and global artisans, examining every dimension with intention and anticipating challenges like a chess match to ensure each project progresses flawlessly.

For Arpan Gupta, building is more than construction — it is an art form. That vision has earned the firm national recognition, including multiple gold and silver wins at the 2025 NAHB Awards, four top honors at the 2025 Texas Association of Builders Awards, the 2025 LUXE Interiors + Design RED Award, and the 2024 & 2025 GHBA PRISM Grand Award for Custom Builder of the Year.

Construction Excellence

The Build

At Fifty Seventh & 7th Luxury Homes, attention to detail guides every stage of construction. Our team conducts daily on-site inspections of subcontractor work to ensure that each phase meets the highest standards of quality and precision.

By maintaining this level of oversight throughout the building process, we keep the final vision clearly in focus. We also take on a limited number of projects at a time, allowing us to dedicate meaningful time and care to each client and each home.

The result is a thoughtful, highly attentive building experience that delivers exactly what you envision, executed with the highest level of craftsmanship and service.
