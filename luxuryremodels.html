Luxury Remodeling in Houston

At Fifty Seventh & 7th Luxury Homes, our expertise extends beyond new construction. We bring the same commitment to excellence, precision, and elevated design to luxury remodel projects — transforming existing homes into extraordinary residences.

Whether you're reimagining a kitchen, creating a spa-inspired bathroom, adding an entertainment suite, or undertaking a comprehensive whole-home renovation, our team approaches every remodel with the same rigor and attention to detail that has earned us national recognition.

What We Transform

Remodel Services

Kitchen Transformations

Chef-inspired redesigns with high-end designer appliances, custom cabinetry, natural stone counters, and bespoke islands that become the heart of your home.

Spa Bathroom Retreats

Primary bathrooms reimagined as private sanctuaries — natural stone, soaking tubs, steam showers, and fixtures that bring the hotel experience home.

Specialty & Entertainment Rooms

Private screening rooms, wine cellars, wellness suites with saunas, and bespoke entertainment spaces tailored to your lifestyle.

Whole-Home Renovations

Comprehensive renovations that modernize layout, flow, and finishes throughout — bringing your entire home to a new standard of luxury.

Outdoor Living

Covered patios, summer kitchens, pool surrounds, and outdoor entertaining spaces designed for Houston's climate and your lifestyle.

Custom millwork, artisan staircases, coffered ceilings, and layered architectural detailing that elevate every room.

Our Approach

The Same Excellence, Applied to Your Home

Every remodel begins the same way our new builds do — with a thorough review of the space, your vision, and our possibilities. We use the same trusted trade network, the same quality of materials, and the same daily oversight that defines our new construction projects.
